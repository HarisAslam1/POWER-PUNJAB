Services

Engine & Gear
Repairing

We offer comprehensive engine diagnostics and repair for all gasoline, diesel, and hybrid systems, from minor services to full overhauls, ensuring peak performance. Our expert transmission and gearbox service repairs both manual and automatic systems, fixing everything from slipping gears to fluid leaks to guarantee smooth, reliable shifting.

Accident Car
Repairing

We offer full-spectrum collision repair, restoring your vehicle completely from core structural damage (frame, suspension, airbags) using advanced systems and mechanical expertise. We seamlessly handle all auto body, paint, and refinishing work along with free estimates and complete insurance claim assistance to get you back on the road safely.

Auto Painting

Our Professional Auto Painting Service for a stunning, durable finish, featuring computerized color matching for perfect results on everything from panel repair to full-body repaints. We use a meticulous process—including scratch removal and premium clear coats—to guarantee flawless quality that restores your vehicle's value and look.

Auto Electrical

Our specialized Auto Electrical Service handles all vehicle electronics, from essential Starting & Charging Systems (batteries, alternators, starters) to Advanced Diagnostics (ECU, sensors, warning lights). We ensure lasting solutions by expertly repairing wiring, lighting, and all Comfort & Convenience features.

AC Repairing

We restore your comfort with comprehensive Auto AC Service, starting with full diagnostics to precisely locate leaks, blockages, or electrical faults in your system. We expertly handle refrigerant recharge, component replacement (compressor, evaporator), and blower motor/electrical fixes to ensure reliable, maximum cooling.

Detailing Polishing

We provide comprehensive detailing that begins with multi-stage paint correction and polishing to eliminate scratches and restore a deep, mirror-like gloss. We follow through with deep interior rejuvenation (shampooing and leather care) and apply superior protective finishes like ceramic coatings to safeguard your vehicle's showroom shine.
